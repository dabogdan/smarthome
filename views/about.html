About MySmartHome

MySmartHome is a home automation or domotics, the automation for a home, sometimes called a smart home or smart house. MySmartHome automation system will monitor and/or control home attributes such as lighting, climate, entertainment systems, and appliances. It may also include home security such as access control and alarm systems.

The software allows the customization of all your devices as mush as you like.

It conveniently stores the data in the database and allows you full control of your home.
